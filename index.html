I loved solving jigsaw puzzles as a kid. Something about making all the small puzzle pieces form a bigger picture clicked and left me satisfied. During my adolescence I grew out of puzzles and found the same satisfaction when building large scale Lego kits. And naturally, I grew out of that and was in the search for a new outlet for that same passion. That new outlet became programming.

As a fourth year attending UCSD, I now am majoring in Cognitive Science, focusing on Machine Learning and Neural Computation. I am glad to say that I finally found my path in life and still carry that passion to this day. I love programming because there is always something new to learn and new obstacles to conquer; there is always room for growth.

Reach out if you ever want to talk about anything!
